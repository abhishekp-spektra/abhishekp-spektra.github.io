Ingram Micro · New content

Unified Security & Compliance — Microsoft Defender XDR + Purview

Delivered on time · 3 runs live

Onboarded brand-new security content against a delivery date the customer couldn't move. With rescheduling off the table, I compressed the plan, rallied the team, and landed the onboarding on schedule — the lab has already been delivered three times.

Post-delivery follow-through: two experience issues surfaced in early runs — a license assignment that upstream validation had marked healthy (assigned and corrected during the event), and by-design result delays in the Purview and Defender portals. For the latter I introduced a pre-configuration pattern: staging the slow, time-bound configurations in advance, so participants perform every step hands-on and still see completed results immediately.
